Privacy Notice

Data Controller: AutoHub Kenya Limited

Information We Collect

In compliance with the Data Protection Act (2019), we collect and process the following personal data:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (for registration)
  • Vehicle preferences and search history (to improve your experience)
  • Cookies for session management and analytics

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your explicit consent when registering
  • Legitimate business interests in providing marketplace services
  • Compliance with legal obligations under Kenyan law

Your Rights

Under the Data Protection Act (2019), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures as required by the Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act (2018) to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact our Data Protection Officer at: dpo@autohubke.com

Office of the Data Protection Commissioner: www.odpc.go.ke
